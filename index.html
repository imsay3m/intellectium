ABOUT INTELLECTIUM

Innovation Starts With Young Minds.

Intellectium is a youth-driven global platform committed to innovation, sustainable development, and civic engagement. Founded with the vision of empowering changemakers, Intellectium provides a collaborative space for young minds to ideate, lead, and execute impactful solutions for the world's most pressing challenges.

Our Story

Intellectium was born from the shared vision of four passionate educators and technologists who recognized the urgent need to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving demands of the modern world.

With combined experience spanning over 40 years in education, technology, and community development, our founders bring diverse perspectives and complementary skills that drive our mission forward.

Our Past Events

Celebrating Our Journey of Impact

A look back at the milestones that defined our community's growth and inspired young leaders worldwide.

Completed
August 2, 11 & 23, 2023

Start-Up 101: Launch Your Start-Up

An online workshop series in collaboration with Penny Bee Solutions, designed to teach the fundamentals of entrepreneurship.

Day 1 (Aug 2): Business planning and problem-solving with Faizul Islam Anik

Day 2 (Aug 23): Team building and goal-setting with Shantanu Barua

Aug 11: Business Plan Pitching Competition judged by Faizul Islam, Shantanu Barua, and Pulok Sarker

The event empowered aspiring entrepreneurs to develop, pitch, and refine their start-up ideas through expert guidance and practical application.

Completed
October 5-7, 2023

Mastering Artificial Intelligence

A 3-day interactive workshop where participants explored AI tools like OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google Bard.

Day 1: Introduction to Conversational AI and prompt writing

Day 2: Creating images, videos, audio, and presentations using AI.

Day 3: AI Image Manipulation Competition with participant submissions and winner announcements.

The event combined learning and creativity, equipping attendees with practical AI skills.

Completed
November 25, 2023

BizTech Talent Showcase Competition

An exciting competition featuring two segments:

1: Business Plan Pitching - Participants presented innovative business ideas.

2: Technology Talent Showcase - Creators demonstrated their latest tech projects.

Judged by industry professionals, the event celebrated creativity, innovation, and talent in business and tech.
